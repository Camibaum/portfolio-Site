The Solution
Throughout this project, I developed skills in both design and coding. On the design side, I learned how to research and ideate around a specific theme, create visually appealing and functional wireframes, and develop a digital prototype in Figma. I gained experience in presenting my ideas and refining my work based on usability testing and feedback. On the coding side, I built a responsive website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, focusing on mobile-first development, usability, and adherence to web conventions.

Website

Process

The project began with thorough research and ideation, where I identified a meaningful theme for the workbook site. I explored potential user needs and collected relevant text and images to support my vision. From there, I created initial wireframes and developed a clickable prototype in Figma. This prototype underwent usability testing, which helped identify areas for improvement.

Building the coded version, I implemented the refined design into a functional site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on responsive and mobile-first design. Throughout the process, I documented my work using Figma, FigJam, and a logbook, reflecting on each phase. This approach allowed me to refine both the visual and functional aspects of the project and present a cohesive solution.
