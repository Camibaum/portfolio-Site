The Solution
Together as a group we did a brand redesign of a Danish jewelry company’s website, which has a proud tradition of craftsmanship passed down through generations. The project included rebranding the company’s identity by creating a new logo, color palette, and typography.

We produced engaging content, including video interviews, LottieFiles animations, and photographs. The final solution was a responsive and visually appealing website that effectively communicated the company's heritage and craftsmanship.

Process

The process began with planning and project management using tools like Trello and SCRUM. My team and I collaborated on defining goals and establishing a clear workflow. After selecting the company, we conducted research on its current website, analyzing the brand, target audience, user journey, and site structure.

We created a storyboard and sketches to plan the video interview, ensuring a clear narrative and visual style. The filming process involved capturing high-quality footage with proper lighting and sound. In postproduction,

Parallel to this, we developed wireframes and prototypes in Figma, ensuring the website’s layout and functionality met the design goals. Coding the website involved using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, following a mobile-first approach. Finally we conducted a 5-seconds testing of the site, as well as using other tools like Lighthouse and user testing methods to ensure optimal performance and usability. The project was finalised by a group report with our insights and learnings
