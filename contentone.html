The Solution
'Scenekunst is a creative project where I explored both design and coding disciplines by producing a video interview and developing a website. The project showcases the actor Laura and her passion for theatre and stage performances from a young age in a 2-min video. I captured her story through video, edited it to highlight her connection to the art of performance, and created a visually engaging website that embodies the essence of theatre.

Process

The process began with extensive preproduction planning, which included developing the concept, writing interview questions, and preparing a storyboard and sketches to structure the interview and ensure a clear visual direction. This step was crucial for capturing the actor’s story authentically while maintaining a consistent narrative flow. During filming, I focused on technical aspects such as camera operation, lighting, and audio recording to produce high-quality footage that aligned with the project’s vision.

Postproduction & Website

In postproduction, I edited the video to create a cohesive and compelling narrative, integrating b-roll footage and incorporating enhancements like color correction, audio adjustments, and text overlays to reflect the theatrical theme. After completing the video, I moved on to designing and coding the website. I began by creating detailed wireframes to define the layout and user flow. From there, I built a responsive website that integrated the video seamlessly, combining visuals and interactivity to evoke the elegance and emotion of theatre.
